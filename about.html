Tamil Christian TV Stations (TCTVS)

TCTVS is a Christ-centered mobile application that brings live Tamil Christian television content directly to your fingertips.

Our mission is to support the global Tamil Christian community by providing easy access to sermons, worship, Bible teachings, and family-friendly programs from trusted ministries.

Whether you're at home, traveling, or in a region with limited Christian media, TCTVS helps you stay connected to the Body of Christ through the power of digital broadcasting.
